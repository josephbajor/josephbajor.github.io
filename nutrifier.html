The Nutifier is a pacifier designed to provide nourishment to malnourished infants. It is being developed for Nutrivide, a startup I am working on with a few others within the Honors College innovation lab. I oversee both the mechanical and aesthetic design of the device and have also created the company logo. You can visit our website here.

The Nutrifier was made in Solidworks.

The design showcased is outdated, but still features some clever design choices, such as incorporating the air intake valves into the logo on the front. It was designed with a user-removeable nutrient chamber.
