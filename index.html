My name is Joseph Bajor. I am currently a student at Rutgers University, as well as an inagural member of the Honors College. I have always been interested in engineering and design, and have used my free time here to develop the skills needed to realize some of my most ambitious ideas. This website showcases many of my personal projects, as well as work I have completed for other companies.

Design

All good projects start with a good design, which is why I always start with an aesthetic mockup to guide the development process. I have been designing since early 2014 and have created concepts for everything from PC cases to pacifiers. I have experience with product design, architectural design, typography, logo design, and 3D design. I am also experienced with the entire Adobe creative suite, Blender, and 3Ds Max

Engineering/Prototyping

The majority of the projects I work on eventually must exist in the real world, which means much of my work has to do with creating physical prototypes and proof of concepts. I am a 3D printing professional and have extensive experience with FDM machines. I also have experience with mechanical, industrial, and electrical engineering, and am proficient in Solidworks and other similar CAD packages

Digital Marketing

Many of my projects require a company image to be built around it, as much of the work I do is for startups. I make sure to create a cohesive aesthetic image that seamlessly blends a brand's logo, product aesthetic, and graphics. I have 6 years of experience as a render artist and make use of Blender, Solidworks Visualize, and HDR Lightroom for most of my imagery
